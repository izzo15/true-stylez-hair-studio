A great beard isn't just about a visit to the chair every month. **How you treat it in the 27 days between cuts defines whether you look like a client or a professional.** Here's Jonathan's daily routine.

## The 3-Step Home Maintenance Routine

### 1. Wash It (Every 2–3 Days)
- Use a **beard-specific shampoo** (not bar soap — it strips natural oils).
- Massage throughly and rinse with slightly cool water.
- Let it air-dry before applying any product — wet beard absorbs oil unevenly.

### 2. Condition It (Daily Application)
- Apply **beard oil** while your beard is still slightly damp.
- 3–4 drops is usually enough for medium-length beards.
- Comb through to distribute the oil from root to tip.

### 3. Shape It (Every Morning)
- Use a quality **beard comb** for longer beards or a **boar brush** for shorter styles.
- Run it upward and outward to train the hairs in the right direction.

## Trim Shaven Lines at Home
If you're keeping a sharp cheek and neck line, a cheap **single-blade trimmer** ($15–$20) does the trick. Just follow your existing line — don't try to reshape it at home. Save the full line-up for True Stylez.
